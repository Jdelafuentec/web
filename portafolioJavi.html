Javiera de la Fuente

Designer and researcher specialized in technologies for conservation and territorial development. I develop digital tools, monitoring systems, and collaborative projects that connect science, local culture, and applied innovation.

Blink+A

Blink+A is a non-lethal experimental device that uses light to deter carnivores and reduce human–wildlife conflict. It was co-developed with local communities and researchers to protect both livestock and wild cats.

Elün

Elün is a participatory conservation initiative that combines technology and community knowledge. It showcases innovative ways to integrate science, storytelling, and local culture for coexistence.

YouTube Video

Olten

Olten is a remote animal monitoring system designed to prevent overgrazing in vast rangelands. It provides real-time data to support sustainable livestock management and healthier ecosystems.

Torres del Paine Biosphere Reserve

The Conservation Action Plan 2024–2030 sets strategies to preserve biodiversity in Patagonia. It links science, policy, and local engagement to ensure the resilience of one of Chile’s most iconic landscapes.

OASIS

OASIS is the Observatory of Andean Salt Flats through Interdisciplinary Studies. It applies satellite monitoring, field research, and community collaboration to understand and protect fragile salt flat ecosystems.
